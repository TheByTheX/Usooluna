About

Usooluna (أصولنا), meaning "our origins," embarks on a humble journey back to where we came from. It's not a quest for status or grand narratives, but a quiet exploration of the stories that nurtured us to connect the family ties. Anas ibn Malik reported that the Messenger of Allah, may Allah bless him and grant him peace, said, "Anyone who wants to have his provision expanded and his term of life prolonged should maintain ties of kinship." (Al-Adab Al-Mufrad 56)

"Whoever recognizes Allah’s favor upon him and thanks Him for it has truly increased it; and whoever denies Allah’s favor upon him and is ungrateful for it has truly diminished it." Tracing our lineage isn't simply about names and dates, but about recognizing the blessings passed down through generations, from parents to grandparents, and back to the very source of our existence.

أصولنا welcomes you on this journey of gratitude and self-discovery. It's a space to celebrate our heritage, acknowledge its challenges, and learn from the experiences that shaped our ancestors and, in turn, shaped us. Whether you're a seasoned genealogist or simply curious about your family history, this is a platform to connect with your roots and embrace the gift of belonging.
